Pasta Dough

  • Time:about 45 minutes
  • Rating:5 Stars
  • Notes: Read Community Notes

Fresh pasta isn't something to master in one go. It takes time and patience but it yields dividends. This particular recipe is vastly verstile. It can be made into whole grain pasta by swapping in 1 cup sifted whole wheat, spelt or farro flour in place of 1 cup all purpose or 00 flour. Add more egg yolks or water as needed and the rest of the dough for one hour. Or try a green pasta, as in this ravioli verdi: Steam or saute 6 ounces baby spinach (about 6 cups) until just wilted. spread it out on a parchment-lined baking sheet, an, when cool, squeeze water out thoroughly, a handful at a time, then chop roughly. Puree with 2 eggs and 1 egg yolk, then use this mixture in place of eggs in the recipe. Or for something a little different, make a herbed pasta, like this pappardelle, by stirring in 1/2 cup finely chopped parsely, chives, chervil, tarragon or basil in any combination to the eggs before adding the flour in the main recipe.

Preparation

  1. Mound the flour in a wide mixing bowl
  2. Beat together eggs
  3. Mix eggs with flour
  4. Transfer to counter surface and knead
  5. Rest dough for 30 minutes
