Deals of the Week

Adventure Horizon Theme Park

Welcome to Adventure Horizon Theme Park where excitiement and adventure await at every turn! Locataed in the heart of scenic landscapes, Adventure Horizon offers an unforgettable experience for visitors of all ages. Our park features a thrilling array of world class rides, immersive themed areas and spectacular live entertainment that guarantees a day full of fun and wonder.

  1. Sky Surge Roller Coaster: soar to new heights on our iconic Sky Surge Roller Coaster. Feel the rush of adrenaline as you twist and turn through breathtaking loops and exhilarating drops.
  2. Jungle Safari Adventure: Embark on a wild journey through our Jungle Safari Adventure. Encounter lifelike animatronic animals and navigate through lush, tropical environments that make you feel like you're on a real safari.
  3. Wave Pool: Dive into our Wave Pool, the perfect to cool off and catch some waves. Whether you want to relax on a float or ride the surf, the Wave Pool is a hit with all ages.
